-> Airline Customer Satisfaction:

1. Problem Statement: Predicting airline customer satisfaction based on various factors to enhance service quality and customer experience.

2. Goal: Develop a machine learning model that accurately predicts customer satisfaction with airline services, achieving an accuracy of at least 80%.

3. Metrics: Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1-score.

4. Stakeholders: Airline management, customer service department, marketing team, passengers.

5. Data Sources:
    - Historical customer feedback and survey data provided by the airline.
    - Flight records including departure/arrival times, delays, cancellations, and routes.

6. Constraint: Limited budget and resources for data collection and analysis.

7. Assumption: 
    - The provided data represents diverse demographics and flight experiences across various routes and classes.
    - The customer feedback accurately reflects their satisfaction levels.
